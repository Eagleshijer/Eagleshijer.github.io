ABOUTS US

Angela Peace and Love Company Limited, trading as Beauty Group, is a company registered under the law of Tanzania in 2018. Its objectives are, among others, to carry on the business of importing and selling new and second-hand clothes and shoes. The company was incorporated as a result of the expansion of Beauty Group, which was established in China in 2012 and expanded to various African countries such as Tanzania, Kenya, Uganda, and Congo. The company`s headquarter is in Dar es Salaam, Keko. The company has business branches in Arusha, Mwanza, Kasulu, Zanzibar, Iringa, Mbeya, Songea, Morogoro, Dodoma, Keko, and Kariakoo.
